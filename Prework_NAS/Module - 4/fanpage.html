Bio

Able Tesfaye AKA The Weeknd is a candian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is known for his falsetto and electric sounding music. He is heavly influenced by pop, R&B, and Hip Hop music. He began is recording career in 2010, anonymously uploading several songs to a youtube account. Nobody knew who he was but his music was gaining steam. A year later, he released the mixtapes House of Baloons, Thursday, and Echoes of Silence and quickly earned a following and critical recognition from several mainstream publications. His music was a dark style of R&B and there was a mystique surrounding his identity since up till that point he never showed who he was.

Since then, he has released 4 studio albums and has won three Grammy Awards, eight Billboard Music Awards, five American Music Awards, two MTV Video Music Awards, nine Juno Awards, and has been nominated for an Academy Award. He has proven to be very successful and is still striving to be the best and expand his music.
